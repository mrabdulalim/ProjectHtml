Cyber Security





What are the consequences of losing data?

  • Data exploitation – Sensitive information such as banking data, social security numbers can be misused
  • Financial fraud – A hacker may gain access to a person’s bank account and commit fraudulent acts
  • Identity theft – A cyber criminal can impersonate someone if they can manipulate their personal details
  • Website defacement – This will tarnish an organisations’ reputation and takes away their controls
  • Loss of clients – If a business gets hacked and lose clients’ data, this will naturally cause customers to leave
  • Information leak – A user’s personal information such as names and addresses could be leaked to the public and put them in harm
  • Reputational damage - Any hack will harm the organisation's status
  • Negative impact on business’ profits - Businesses will lose trust if their services are hacked therefore decreasing profits
